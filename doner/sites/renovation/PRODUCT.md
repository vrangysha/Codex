# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners and apartment owners who are planning turnkey renovation and need a contractor they can evaluate quickly: trust signals, scope clarity, pricing orientation, process transparency, and a low-friction way to request a consultation.

## Product Purpose

Create a modern multi-page marketing site for turnkey apartment and house renovation services. Success means visitors understand the offer, see a credible structure for services and process, and submit qualified leads through accessible forms.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, accountable. The site should feel like a careful site manager: clear about constraints, confident about process, and respectful of budget and home context.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic construction templates, aggressive discount banners, fake social proof, dark luxury real-estate styling, AI-purple lead-gen clichés, beige/cream renovation brochure palettes, and any invented cases, reviews, or project photos presented as real.

## Design Principles

- Trust is built through specificity: scope, process, constraints, and source labels beat vague promises.
- Lead generation must stay respectful: short forms, visible contact paths, and clear expectations after submission.
- Light does not mean washed out: readability, contrast, and hierarchy take priority over decorative subtlety.
- Placeholder content is allowed only when marked as placeholder and tracked for owner replacement.
- SEO structure should support humans first: pages, headings, schema, and internal links must mirror real service decisions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, semantic landmarks, readable contrast, reduced motion, descriptive alt text for meaningful assets, and mobile touch targets of at least 44px.
