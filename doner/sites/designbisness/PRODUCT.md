# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Owners of apartments, houses, and small commercial spaces who want a calm premium interior without managing dozens of contractors themselves. They are usually choosing a studio before renovation starts, comparing trust, taste, process transparency, and budget clarity.

## Product Purpose

AURUM is a multi-page landing site for an interior design studio. The site should turn visual trust into a concrete next step: view portfolio styles, choose a service package, take a style quiz, understand the process, order author supervision, or submit a detailed project brief.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, tactile, exacting. The brand should feel like a finished interior where every material has a reason: warm but not sentimental, premium but not loud, practical without becoming utilitarian.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic beige template landing pages, over-decorated luxury, purple-blue gradients, glassmorphism, icon-card grids repeated without hierarchy, fake metrics, and stock "designer at desk" imagery. Do not make the site look like a SaaS dashboard or a real-estate catalog.

## Design Principles

1. Show spaces before explaining them: imagery should carry the first impression.
2. Make process feel controlled: timelines, packages, and forms must reduce uncertainty.
3. Let typography behave like architecture: strong scale, measured rhythm, no visual noise.
4. Use restraint with authority: black actions, thin rules, warm material accents, and precise spacing.
5. Treat forms as consultation tools, not lead traps: every question should feel useful.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain readable contrast on light surfaces, keyboard access for navigation, quiz, FAQ, and forms, visible focus states, 44px touch targets, semantic labels, descriptive alt text, and reduced-motion alternatives.
